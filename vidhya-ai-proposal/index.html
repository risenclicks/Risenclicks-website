10. STRATEGIC INSIGHTS (CRITICAL)

Tier 1 (High Competition)

  • High CPM due to big EdTech players
  • Requires strong creatives + premium positioning

Tier 2 (Sweet Spot)

  • Best ROI potential
  • High volume + moderate cost
  • Faster decision-making audience

Tier 3 (High Engagement)

  • High CTR, low CPC
  • Lower paid conversion
  • WhatsApp critical for trust

11. TRAFFIC vs LEADS STRATEGY

Traffic Campaigns

  • Lower CPC
  • Best for:
  • Creative testing
  • Awareness

Leads Campaigns

  • Higher CPC
  • Better intent
  • Meta optimizes for conversions

Strategy for Vidhya AI

Start with Traffic (testing phase) ➔ Move to Leads (scaling phase)
